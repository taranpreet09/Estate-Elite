About Loan Calculator

Know how a bank provides a deal for their customers

A home loan calculator helps estimate the monthly payments a borrower needs to make based on the total loan amount. This tool enables you to make informed decisions regarding the monthly outflow required to repay your home loan.

To calculate your home loan, you'll need to provide the following information:

  • Loan Amount: The total loan amount you wish to borrow for your property.
  • Loan Tenure: The length of time (in years) over which you plan to repay the loan.
  • Interest Rate: The rate of interest applied to your loan.
