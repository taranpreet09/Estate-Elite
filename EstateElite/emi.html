About EMI Calculator

Know how a bank provides a deal for their customers

A home loan EMI calculator helps estimate the monthly payments a borrower needs to make based on the total loan amount. This tool enables you to make informed decisions regarding the monthly outflow required to repay your home loan.

To calculate your home loan EMI, you'll need to provide the following information:

  • Loan Amount: The total loan amount you wish to borrow for your property.
  • Loan Tenure: The length of time (in years) over which you plan to repay the loan. A longer tenure can reduce your monthly EMI.
  • Interest Rate: The rate of interest applied to your loan.

Frequently asked questions about EMI Calculator

Know what questions the users frequently ask about EMI calculators

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment, a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each month.

A home loan EMI is the monthly payment you make to repay your home loan over a specified tenure.

Pre-EMI refers to the interest you pay on the loan amount disbursed, before the actual EMI payments begin.

Pre-EMI can be a good option if you're not immediately ready to move into the property but want to keep payments low during the construction phase.
